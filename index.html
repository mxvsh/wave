Features

Built to stay out of your way

Every detail designed for one thing — getting your words out fast, naturally, and privately.

One-key activation

Hold Right ⌥ Option in any app and start speaking. Release to stop. That's the entire interface.

Completely private

Whisper runs fully on-device. Your voice never leaves your Mac — no cloud, no servers, no data collection. Ever.

Streams to your cursor

Text appears directly where your cursor is — no clipboard, no paste step. Just natural, flowing text as you speak.

Groq for speed

Switch to Groq for ultra-fast real-time transcription when you need it — blazing responses with near-zero latency.

Works offline

No Wi-Fi? No problem. Whisper runs entirely locally — fully functional on planes, in basements, anywhere.

Zero friction

No accounts, no sign-ups, no onboarding. Download, run, and dictate. It's ready as fast as you are.

Privacy

Your voice stays yours

Wave was designed with privacy as the foundation — not as a feature.

Local Whisper

OpenAI Whisper runs entirely on your Mac. Audio is processed in memory, never stored, never sent anywhere. Fully offline capable.

Groq option

When speed matters more, switch to Groq's API for near-instant transcription. You supply your own API key — we never see it.

No account. No telemetry. No nonsense.

Wave collects zero user data. No analytics trackers, no crash reporters phoning home, no usage metrics. It's a native app that runs on your machine — full stop. Open source so you can verify every claim.
